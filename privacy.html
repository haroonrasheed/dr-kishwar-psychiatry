Privacy & Disclaimer

How we protect your information and what to know about this website.

Privacy notice

This website provides general information about psychiatric services offered by Dr. Kishwar. It does not collect or store protected health information (PHI). If you submit the contact form, your details are transmitted for scheduling purposes only.

Communications are reviewed during business hours. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal, financial, or medical details through the form; a secure channel will be provided for intake.

Medical disclaimer

Content on this site is for informational purposes and is not medical advice. An evaluation with Dr. Kishwar is required before any diagnosis or treatment recommendations are made.

Use of this site does not establish a patient-physician relationship. Decisions about care should be made between you and your clinician.

Emergency notice

This site is not monitored for emergencies. If you are experiencing a medical or psychiatric emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.
