About Dr. Kishwar

Kishwar Ali, MD - board-certified psychiatrist with 30 years of experience providing compassionate, evidence-based care for patients 12 and older.

Professional background

Dr. Kishwar Ali is an independent, board-certified psychiatrist serving adolescents and adults. She completed medical school at Sindh Medical College and residency at Loyola University Medical Center in Maywood, IL. As a Fellow of the American Psychiatric Association, she is committed to collaborative, measurable progress for every patient.

She offers both telehealth and in-office consultations, and was the first in DuPage County to utilize Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) for adult and adolescent depression and obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD).
